Tab Buddy is a browser extension designed to simplify tab management. It allows you to create and organize pre-set tab combinations, name your tabs, and open them with a single click. Whether for work or leisure, Tab Buddy enhances your browsing experience.

To create pre-set tab combos, click on the "Add to Preset" button, choose a name for your preset, and select the tabs you want to include. You can then open these pre-set tab combinations with a single click.

Yes, you can! Tab Buddy allows you to give custom names to your tabs, making it easy to identify and organize them according to your preferences.

Simply select the desired preset from the "On-Startup Preset" dropdown. Tabs associated with that preset will open automatically every time you start your browser.
